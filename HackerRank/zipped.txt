N, X = map(int, input().split())
marks_list = []
for _ in range(X):
    marks_list += [list(map(float, input().split()))]

for marks in zip(*marks_list):
    print(f"{sum(marks)/X:.1f}")

Title: zipped
Approach: Store marks of each subject in a list, use zip(*marks_list) to group marks student-wise, then compute and print each student’s average.

Time & Space Complexity: Time complexity is O(N × X) and space complexity is O(N × X).
